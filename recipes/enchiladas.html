Enchiladas

Here is a classical display of traditional mexican enchiladas.

Enchiladas best known as a tortilla wrapped around meat such as beef or pork, cheese, and vegetables.

It is a notable mexican cuisine that took inspiration from the aztec and spanish influences.

Que Ingredientes or What ingredients to make this dish?

  • Guajillo peppers
  • Ancho peppers
  • Garlic cloves (chopped)
  • Mexican oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded beef, pork, or chicken
  • Queso fresco crumble
  • White onion (finely chopped)
  • Vegetable oil

Here is how we combine beautiful ingredients

Start with the sauce!

  1. Slightly roast the peppers
  2. Once roasted, place them in a saucepan with water and turn the heat to medium
  3. Remove the saucepan from the stove and let them cool. Then drain the peppers and place them into a blender with the garlic cloves
  4. Add 1/2 cup of clean water and blend until you have a smooth sauce, season with oregano, salt, and pepper

Now time to prep the tortillas

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degreees to keep the enhiladas warm while you finish assembling them
  2. Add the 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Dip the tortilla into the sauce and place it in the frying comal pan or skillet
  4. Briefly fry them for a few seconds on both sides

Assembly time!

  1. To assemble, place the meat filling in the center of the tortilla, and fold it
  2. Repeat the step until you have run out of the meat filling and tortillas
  3. Sprinkle the enchiladas with the cheese crumbled and chopped onions
  4. Add the garnish of your choice and enjoy!
